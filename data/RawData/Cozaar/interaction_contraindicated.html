• aliskiren

    losartan, aliskiren. Either increases toxicity of the other by pharmacodynamic synergism. Contraindicated. Aliskiren use contraindicated with ARBs in patients with diabetes; avoid coadministration with ARBs if GFR <60 mL/min; dual blockade of renin-angiotensin system increases risks of hypotension, hyperkalemia, and renal impairment.
