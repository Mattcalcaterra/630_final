• itraconazole

    itraconazole increases levels of alprazolam by decreasing metabolism. Contraindicated.

  • ketoconazole

    ketoconazole increases levels of alprazolam by decreasing metabolism. Contraindicated.

  • sodium oxybate

    alprazolam, sodium oxybate. Either increases effects of the other by pharmacodynamic synergism. Contraindicated. Prescribing information for sodium oxybate states coadministration with alcohol or insomnia drugs is contraindicated because of additive CNS depression.

  • tipranavir

    tipranavir increases levels of alprazolam by affecting hepatic/intestinal enzyme CYP3A4 metabolism. Contraindicated. Potential for increased toxicity. Tipranavir is used with ritonavir (boosted therapy) which is a potent CYP3A4 inhibitor.
